### 📝 Introduction
> "Data is the new oil, but statistics is the engine that refines it."

This course focuses on the core principles of probability and statistical analysis that are fundamental to Data Engineering. As I progress toward a career in data architecture, understanding how to interpret data distributions and perform hypothesis testing is essential for making data-driven decisions and building reliable models.

---

## 📚 Syllabus & Topics
Here is the breakdown of the chapters we covered this semester:

| Chapter | Topic | Key Concepts |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 1️⃣ | **Introduction to Statistics** | Population vs Sample, types of data, and sampling methods. |
| 2️⃣ | **Data Description** | Visualizing data using histograms, box plots, and frequency tables. |
| 3️⃣ | **Descriptive Statistics** | Measures of central tendency (Mean/Median) and variation. |
| 4️⃣ | **Probability & Distributions** | Random variables, Normal, Binomial, and Poisson distributions. |
| 5️⃣ | **Hypothesis Testing** | P-values, Z-tests, T-tests, and making statistical inferences. |
